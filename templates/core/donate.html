Give Now

Transparency in Action: Every Penny Counts

100% of your public donation goes directly to animals in need. Paws a Moment Rescue is a dedicated group of community members making a difference to abandoned, neglected, and unwanted dogs and cats of the Hunter Region. Every penny goes toward essential expenses:

Feeding, housing, life-saving vet care, rehabilitation, transport, and adoption support.

Where Your Donation Goes:

  • $25: Provides a week's supply of specialised food for a sick kitten.
  • $50: Covers vaccinations and microchipping for one adopted pet.
  • $150: Funds essential vet checks and initial flea/worm treatments.
  • $500: Contributes to a major surgery or long-term rehabilitation for a pet with complex needs.
