Our Story

Driven by Compassion

Paws a Moment and Rescue is a volunteer-run, non-profit animal rescue based on the Central Coast, NSW. We don't have a central shelter; instead, our animals live in real homes with loving foster carers.

Our mission is simple: to provide a safety net for animals in need and to find them the perfect "match" for their forever homes. We believe every animal deserves a second chance and a moment of peace.

Our Values:

Individualized care, thorough vetting, and lifelong support for our adopters.
