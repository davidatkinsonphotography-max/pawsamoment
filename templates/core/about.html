Our Story

Driven by Compassion

We are a very small group of dedicated volunteers who assist in rehoming animals in need.

All the animals who come into care are placed in loving foster homes while they await vet work to be done and in some cases, rehabilitation.

Once the animal is ready for adoption, they will be advertised via our FB page and also Pet Rescue to find their forever home.

Adopters will be asked to provide answers to a range of questions so that we can be assured they are going to the best homes. Yard/house checks may also be carried out prior to an adoption.

We also aim to educate the public on responsible pet ownership and the importance of desexing your pets. To reduce the number of Dogs and Cats euthanised each year in Pounds/Shelters.

All our animals available for adoption will come Microchipped, Vaccinated, Desexed, Wormed and Flea Treated. They will all have had a complete vet check prior to adoption.

Our Values:

Individualized care, thorough vetting, and lifelong support for our adopters.
