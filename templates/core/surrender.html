Surrendering your pet

If you need to surrender your pet, we would like to help rehome.

We Are Here to Help

We understand that sometimes life circumstances change. As a foster-based rescue, we do not have a physical kennel facility. This means we can only take in animals when we have an available foster home.

The Surrender Process:

  1. Contact us via email with photos and a description of the animal.
  2. We will search for a suitable foster placement.
  3. If a home is found, we will arrange a temperament assessment.

If you need to surrender an animal, please email us directly at pawsamomentandrescue@gmail.com with as much detail as possible.
